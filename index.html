Dr. Christian Beilschmidt

Hi, I'm Christian Beilschmidt

Computer Scientist, Entrepreneur & Speaker

I'm a Computer Scientist from Germany with an interest in geo data processing and systems design. I'm a co-founder of Geo Engine GmbH and was previously researcher at the University of Marburg.

Geo Engine is a cloud-based analysis platform that overcomes the limitations of existing solutions on the market. It connects data sources in such a way that data scientists can process them interactively over time intervals and analyze them visually. The results can be integrated into machine learning frameworks and business processes via our interfaces or presented to decision makers as an interactive app.

www.geoengine.de
